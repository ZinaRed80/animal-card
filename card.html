Donkey

Donkeys are very smart and have incredibly good balance, they understand the weight of their cargo and how it affects their stride.

  • Scientifc Name: Equus asinus
  • Average Hight: 2.6 - 5.2 ft.
  • Average Lifespan: 27 - 40 years
  • Habiat: Deserts and Savannas

To cool down donkeys use their ears when they get overheated, when it comes to eating donkeys are very eager to eat, they can eat up to 6,000 pounds of food per year. Male donkeys are called jacks and females are called jennets/jennies.
